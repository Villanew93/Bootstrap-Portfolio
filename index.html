About Me

My all time favorite hobbie is going to concerts. When it comes to music I am very open minded and would attend any concert. Recently, started getting into surfing, challenging but worth learning. Not sure, but there is peace being alone in the waves. Currently I am stuck between two dream jobs or careera. I would love to teach English and Spanish abroad, preferably in Japan. I believe in order to progress in life you need to adapt to modern life, including technology. The challenge is what keeps me going, as well as the opportunites this career will offer once I graduate from this course. I see a better and brighter ahead learning this new material.
